Basketball Stars Unblocked Game

Experience the ultimate basketball action in Basketball Stars! Play as legendary players like Lebron James and Stephen Curry, brought to you by Madpuffers. Whether you're a solo player or competing with friends, this HTML5 sequel to Basketball Legends on Poki offers the best two-player basketball experience. Master the game's controls, including WASD for Player One and Arrow keys for Player Two, and showcase your skills in quick matches or full tournaments. Show that you have what it takes to shine in the NBA!

How to Play Basketball Stars?

Player One

  • WASD - Move
  • B - Shoot/Perform action
  • S - Pump
  • V - Super-shot
  • D twice - Dash

Player Two

  • Arrow keys - Move
  • L - Shoot/Perform action
  • DOWN Arrow - Pump
  • K - Super-shot
  • LEFT Arrow twice - Dash
